A digital garden is a form of [[pkm|PKM (Personal Knowledge Management)]].

## Related Concepts

- [[zettelkasten|Zettelkasten]] - A method of linking small notes. The root of digital gardens
- **Second Brain** - A system for storing and organizing thoughts externally
- **Learn in Public** - Sharing the learning process openly

## The Difference

Digital gardens emphasize "public". Most PKM systems are personal, but gardens are built for sharing from the start.

By sharing:
- You organize your thoughts more clearly
- You can receive feedback
- You connect with people who share similar interests
